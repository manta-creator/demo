Access Control

Two roles.
Right access.

Reps see their own data. Managers see everything. Role-based access enforced at every API call.

Role
Rep
  • Own leads only
  • Log calls & SMS
  • Personal KPIs
  • Calendar sync
Role
Manager
  • All reps' data
  • Reassign leads
  • Team KPI view
  • Pipeline report
What's Built

Full-stack
feature set.

Everything a life insurance team needs — nothing they don't.

📋
Lead Management
Create, edit, search, and filter leads. Assign ownership. Track every status change automatically.
📞
Click-to-Call via Telnyx
One tap initiates a call. Outcome and notes logged after. ~70% cheaper than Twilio.
💬
SMS Outreach
Send texts directly from the lead detail page. All messages logged to the activity timeline.
📅
Calendar Integration
Google Calendar sync + Calendly webhook support. Bookings auto-create events linked to a lead.
📊
KPI Tracking
Calls, appointments, and closes tracked per rep per day. Aggregatable by week and month.
🏢
Multi-Org Isolation
Every organization's data is fully isolated. No crossover. Enforced at every query.
